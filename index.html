About Me

I live outside Baltimore, Maryland with my husband and two cats.
I have most recently worked in education and psychiatry, most specifically in behaviors of children and adults with autism and intellectual disabilities. No only have i developed great physical reflexes, but I have been able to learn new skills and even earn a promotion.
I have also completed an independent LEAN project for my unit when I worked at Sheppard Pratt Hospital. I worked for a few months to see how to streamline our process of collecting behavioral data. This included lots of fun data input and spreadsheet creation.
My day-to-day, before COVID-19, inovled a lot of quick thinking. As a Behavior Coach, I worked closely with direct-care staff to train them on how best to interact with students who had IEPs and behavior plans. This often included only a few days of observation and then modeling and adapting as the days passed.
My hobbies include a lot of dressing up in garb from various historical eras. Though I am partial to 1480s Venice.
I also enjoy writing in my free time! I self-published a novel back in 2015.
Currently, I am serializing a novel on Tapas.
It's called Let Them Grumble: Or How the Villainess Gets Revenge and Marries the Duke.
