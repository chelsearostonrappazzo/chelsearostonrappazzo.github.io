About Me

At Work

I live outside Baltimore, Maryland with my husband and two cats.
I grew up in a military family, so we moved around a lot. This coupled with ingrained curiosity has led me to being open to trying all sort of next experiences. I try my best to be flexible in all parts of my life which has served me well in the positions I have held.
I have most recently worked in education and psychiatry, specifically in behaviors of children and adults with autism and intellectual disabilities. Not only have I developed superhuman physical reflexes, but I have been able to learn new skills that help in analyzing the world around me.
I completed an independent LEAN project for my unit when I worked at Sheppard Pratt Hospital. I worked for a few months to see how to streamline our process of collecting behavioral data. This included lots of fun data input and spreadsheet creation.

At Play

My hobbies include a lot of dressing up in garb from various historical era, though I am partial to 1480s Venice. Aside from costuming events, I enjoy wine tasting, Kpop, hot jazz and historical murder mystery shows.
